Sports Predictor

How do I use this site?

First, select the sport you would like to analyze

Second, select the regular season week you would like to predict

*playoff games cannot be predicted and weeks in seasons that have not started cannot be predicted

If the week you chose has already been predicted by the algorithm, then you should see your results quickly

Otherwise the machine learning will take about 5-10 minutes to generate your results
